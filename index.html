ABOUT PROJECT

Our project, GUNSLINGER presents a digital adaptation of the classic board game BANG! utilizing advanced game development technologies. This 3D multiplayer game, developed in Unity and powered by the Fish-Net networking solution, offers an engaging experience where players can compete in real-time. To ensure seamless gameplay even when players disconnect, we integrated sophisticated AI components that take over, maintaining the game's integrity and continuity.
